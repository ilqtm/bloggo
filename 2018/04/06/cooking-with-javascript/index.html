Loop to the end

Cooking is great. I love finding new recipes. I don’t like scrolling through ten pages of a blog on why a recipe matters to a stranger or the recipe’s history. Most times I just want the list and method.

So, I’ve been inspecting my favorite cooking sites to find what element contains the recipe. Once I know that I build a loop in javascript to iterate through that delicious recipe holding div (usually).
The recipe is spit out in plain html on a new tab and I get to avoid searching for it through the blog post and ads.

Once I have a few more sites/loops made I’m going to wrap it into a chrome extension so I don’t have to use the console.

Ultimately, I’d like to have something that will be able to recognize what a recipe like thing is and then spit that out w/o needing all the manual stuff up front.
