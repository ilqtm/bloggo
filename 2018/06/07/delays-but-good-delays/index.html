the good, the bad, and the delayed

The good is that I have a couple of interviews coming up. One is in operations for a role that will transition from mainly sysAd to some field in ops; sec, dev, or sys. Any way it goes I’ll be stoked, it’s for a good team. Here’s hoping!

The bad, this is mostly the cause of what’s been delayed, the septic backed up and the basement bathroom erupted w/ blackwater. After talking w/ a plumber and discovering they bill $300/hr to dig, I got out my favorite tool: a cutter mattock. A shovel, a hoe, and two holes totalling in ~33.5 cubic feet later I was able to work with the plumber to fix the issue (a clogged biofilter in desperate need of replacing).

I installed a riser and lid so I won’t have to dig down 2.2ft next time, added some concrete to a tank transfer pipe for support, and filled it all back in w/ a little help from my gf. Now it’s time to tear out the contaminated bathroom materials and bleach all the things.

The delayed. Literally everthing else. Repairs have taken my attention normally spent on distractions and the septic was a calatlyst for this. Instead of wasting time on reddit et al, I’m limbing trees, fixing a leaky sink or squeaking door, and always, always studying and exploring tech stuff. The bird cam stack is decided for now too.

It’s going on the pi 3 and I’ll be using Motion to control the ps3 eye camera. Project BirdingCam. This is back burner though ‘cause of repairs and interviews.

time blocking

I’ve started time blocking my days as a schedule. It’s working. It’s like pomodoro, but w/o the goofy timer. I just use my phone and a whiteboard. Max time for any single activity atm is an 55 mins, minimum is 25, and transition times are 5 mins. Getting started slow at 5:30am is helping me get a lot done in the day w/ the blocking schedule.

Right now I make the schedule for the day at 7 am or so after I’m done with my morning routine, but I think there is room for improvement. My goal is to create the next day’s schedule at the end of the current day during my wind down period starting at 9pm.

If I’m not finished w/ something and it’s at a crux point, I’ll eat into the next block until a good stopping point. I keep a mid afternoon block for finishing any previous block’s tasks if not finished. If everything is done and I get to my grace block, I just read or find something that needs fixing.

It breaks down a bit when the gf is around, but I think I can compensate for this by just scheduling those blocks as open. This helps me continue to respect my scheduled goals by adhearing to my committments, but allows for time when she arrives home to be open. We’ll see how it goes.

That’s it for now, I’m eating into the next block! It’s just for emails anyway…

So long.
